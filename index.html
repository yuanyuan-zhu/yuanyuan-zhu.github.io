Our team welcome PhD students, master students and outstanding undergraduates from computer Science, mathematics, and other related majors to join the research group for study and research. We will provide students with a good working environment, living allowance, and careful academic guidance to help you explore the cutting-edge scientific research topic, develop the corresponding prototype system and publish high-level academic papers. For outstanding students, including undergraduates, our research group will provide opportunities to go to overseas universities for exchange and study, participate international academic conferences, and continue their studies in well-known universities in the world.

Biography

I am a Full Professor at Computer School, Wuhan University. I received my Ph.D. degree in Computer Science at The Chinese University of Hong Kong in 2013. Before that, I received my bachelor's and master's degrees in Computer Science and Technology from Harbin Institute of Technology in 2007 and 2009 respectively.

Research Interests

My main research areas are Database Techniques, Data Mining, and Data Analysis. Currently I am doing research on Query and Mining in Graph Database, Social Network Analysis, BigData Management and Analysis, and Software Data Mining.
